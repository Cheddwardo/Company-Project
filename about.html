About Us

LeeKeys was founded in 2020 by keyboard enthusiast Bradley Darryl Lee, who struggled to find a suitable keyboard for his professional and gaming needs during the pandemic. Lee quickly realized that there was demand for high quality mechanical keyboards as more people were working from home and on their computers more than ever.

LeeKeys quickly rose in popularity due to its sleek styling and unique keying experience. In 2021 Mariska Hargitay joined Leekeys as Chief Financial Officer and grew the company by 46% and brought in Chief Innovation Officer, Christopher Meloni, who is well known for his design and marketing concepts when it comes to high end keyboards. LeeKeys continues to design and develop innovative products to offer to the keyboard community.
